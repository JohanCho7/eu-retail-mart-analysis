# eu-retail-mart-analysis
Retail sales and profitability dashboard analysis using Tableau

## Overview
The purpose of this study is to analyze sales, profit, the effect of discounts on profits, and consumer behavior in different regions for the European Retail Mart using the Tableau dashboard.

The aim of this data analysis was to examine patterns in the sales and profitability of different categories of products and give recommendations for better business performance.

## Objectives
* Conduct sales and profit performance analysis in different product categories
* Determine the effects of discount on profit performance
* Examine geographical distribution of sales
* Identify consumer behavior trends
* Provide strategic business recommendations

## Tools Used
* Tableau
* Data Visualization
* Business Analytics
* Dashboard Storytelling
